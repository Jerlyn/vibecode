about this vibe

This is a repository of random projects that I vibe code. The purpose is to show projects I've completed in less than a day. I may or may not improve or upgraded them. It allows me to combine user experience design principles with creative development to build interactive projects that are both functional and emotionally engaging.

about me

I'm Jerlyn, an Experience Design Director from the Commonwealth of Dominica with a passion for creating at the intersection of technology and creativity.

As a professional member of the iAAP (I'm CPACC certified) and an Adobe Community Expert, I'm deeply committed to accessible design that works for everyone. When I'm not coding or designing, you might find me reminiscing about past ultramarathons, so I'm training, or working on my next book or illustration.

This portfolio showcases my experiments with generative AI, interactive experiences, and creative coding. I believe in the power of STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Math) to solve problems and create meaningful and delightful experiences.
