Care & Conservation

Preserving Your Investment

Display Recommendations

  • • Avoid direct sunlight
  • • Maintain stable temperature (65-75°F)
  • • Keep humidity levels 45-55%
  • • Use UV-protective glazing if framing

Maintenance

  • • Dust gently with soft brush
  • • Never use cleaning solutions
  • • Professional cleaning when needed
  • • Document any conservation work
